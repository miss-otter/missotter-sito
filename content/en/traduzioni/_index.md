For a couple of years I have been collaborating with the collective blog project [Scritture](https://scritture.nonfiction.it) di nonfiction.it. The texts I select for the project are often articles, short stories or extracts from works whose authors have kindly granted me permission to translate and publish a piece previously unavailable in Italian. For those who would like to get a sense of how I translate, below are the texts I have prepared so far for Scritture.
